About the photographer

A little about the person behind the camera.

I'm a photographer drawn to the quiet, overlooked details that most people walk right past. My focus is on finding beauty in what's often unseen.

Whether I'm documenting a portrait session, covering an event, or exploring a new city while traveling, I approach every shoot the same way: with patience, curiosity, and an eye for the details that make a scene feel real and lasting. Outside of photography, I'm a college student majoring in Economics. My other hobbies include playing video games, travelling, and trying new foods.
